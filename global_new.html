Opel original parts for vehicles across Global markets

This page is an online catalog of original Opel spare parts covering vehicles supplied to many markets worldwide. The list of series and generations shown above is compiled for Global use, which is especially helpful when a car was imported/exported, the destination country is unknown, or the same model was sold in several regions with different configurations. Here you can browse factory parts diagrams, view assemblies in detail and read OEM part numbers to ensure precise selection.

The catalog supports search by model, generation and production years, and also VIN-based selection. Because Global vehicles may vary by emissions rules, engines, lighting, safety packages and trim levels, using the exact generation from the list and decoding the VIN are the most reliable ways to match parts.

Model coverage highlights

The list above spans 33 series and 54 generations, from compact city cars to commercial vans and classics. A few examples to illustrate the scope:

  • City and compact cars: Adam M13 (2013 - 2019), Karl (2016 - 2021), Corsa E (2015 - 2019), Corsa D (2007 - 2014), and the combined entry Corsa B + Tigra A (1993 - 2002).
  • Core compacts: Astra K (2016 - 2021), Astra J (2010 - 2021), Astra H (2004 - 2014), plus the shared platform Astra G + Zafira A (1998 - 2010) and earlier Astra F (1992 - 1998).
  • Mid-size and business class: Insignia B (2018 - 2021) and Insignia A (2009 - 2017), together with the Vectra C (2002 - 2008) lineage.
  • Crossovers and SUVs: Mokka A (2013 - 2019), Crossland X (2017 - 2021), Antara (2007 - 2017), and Frontera B (1999 - 2004).
  • MPV and family models: Zafira C (2012 - 2021), Zafira B (2005 - 2015), Meriva B (2010 - 2017) and Meriva A (2003 - 2010).
  • LCV and passenger vans: Vivaro B (2015 - 2021), Vivaro A (2001 - 2014), Movano B (2010 - 2021), Movano A (1999 - 2010), and Combo D (2012 - 2018).
  • Classic and widely exported models: Kadett E (1985 - 1993), Omega B (1994 - 2003), Omega A (1987 - 1993), Ascona C (1982 - 1988), Calibra A (1989 - 1997).

How to use the catalog

  1. Confirm you are on the Global page. This Global catalog is intended to work across different regions and is ideal when the vehicle’s market specification is uncertain or mixed.
  2. From the list of series above, choose the relevant model family (for example, Astra, Corsa, Insignia, Mokka, Vivaro, Movano).
  3. Select the correct generation and years exactly as shown (for instance, Astra K, 2016 - 2021; Corsa E, 2015 - 2019; Insignia B, 2018 - 2021). Matching years is crucial for Global coverage.
  4. Open the required assembly group: engine, transmission, steering, suspension, brakes, body, interior, electrical, cooling, exhaust, etc.
  5. Use the part diagrams to identify the needed component. Each item has an OEM part number and notes for variants and supersessions.
  6. Copy the OEM number to order parts or verify compatibility. You can also search directly by part number if you already have it.
  7. If specifications differ by market or options, perform a VIN-based search to filter the catalog to the exact configuration of your Opel.
